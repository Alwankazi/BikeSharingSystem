Track Bikes
On this page, you can view all the bike number with its appropriate type of bike in the table provided. You will see an option to filter based on type of bike,based on station,based on reported defective bikes and based on active / stationed and defective, clicking on a certain bike will display the bike location on the map, it will show all the reservations with start end station time and COSt for that bike a filter to see reservations based on future current and ongoing. You will be displayed with an option to cancel reservation if the reservation is future. You will have an option to transfer specific bike to another location at a given time you have the option of replacing the bike at a given time. You will have the option of blocking the bike from viewing in the reservation website
You will be notified if a person does not lock or unlock the bike on time and its your job to cancel the reservations and put a fine on them and you will be notified if the user does not pay the fine you will be notified with the user ID and link to Customer service page and you will be notified if a user marks a bike as defective after their reservation
